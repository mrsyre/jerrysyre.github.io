Rules And Regulations

  1. Attend all classes regularly and punctually.
  2. Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  3. Follow the dress code policy outlined in the school handbook.
  4. No use of electronic devices during class without teacher permission.
  5. Complete and submit assignments on time.
  6. Maintain a clean and organized school environment.
  7. No bullying, harassment, or any form of disruptive behavior.
  8. Adhere to the school's academic honesty and integrity policies.
  9. Use appropriate language and communication in and outside the classroom.
  10. Follow the school's policies on tardiness and absences.
  11. Participate positively in extracurricular activities and events.
  12. No use or possession of drugs, alcohol, or tobacco on school premises.
  13. Adhere to the rules regarding the use of school facilities and equipment.
  14. Follow the guidelines for hallway and cafeteria behavior.
  15. Respect the privacy and personal space of others.
